  2. Regulation of Street Vending) Scheme, 2016 Chhattisgarh Street Vendors (Protection of Livelihood and Regulation of Street Vending) Scheme, 2016

    Terms to be followed:

    • Survey of Street Vendors shall be conducted by the Municipal body for use of the Town Vending Committee ward-wise, trade-wise and vending-zone wise.

    • Every vendor will be required to pay Rs. 100/- (Rupees One Hundred only) to the Town Vending Committee concerned against a proper receipt as fee for issue of the Certificate of Vending. In case of loss or damage of the Certificate, a duplicate shall be issued on payment of Rs. 500/-(Rupees Five Hundreds only).

    • Every vendor who has been granted a Certificate of Vending shall be provided a photo ID Card which shall also bear his Aadhaar number.

  3. Andhra Pradesh Street Vendors (Protection of Livelihood and Regulation of Street Vending) Scheme, 2016

    Criteria to be followed for issuing ID and Vending Certificate:

    • For new application, Ration card or Adhar-card is mandatory, which will be proof for both residential & age proof of 18 years and above.

    • If Ration card/Adhar-card is with old address, Voter-card can be can be taken as residential proof document for getting Residential Vending Certificate.

    • For issue of I D card, Individual passport size photo is sufficient and many more.
